Produced by Blue Sky Studios as its first feature film,
it was released by 20th Century Fox on March 15, 2002.
The film is set during the days of the ice age;
animals begin migrating south to escape the winters.
Once Manny, a wooly mammoth meets Sid, a loudmouthed ground sloth
They find a human baby, and decide to return the baby.
Joining them is a saber-tooth tiger named Diego,
who is commanded by his pack leader to bring the baby to him for
revenge against the humans.

This film was met with mostly positive reviews and was nominated
at the 75th Academy Awards for Best Animated Feature.
